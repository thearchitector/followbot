Sprint 3

For Sprint 3, our goal was to reach our minimal viable product (MVP), a robot that follows a person on flat ground. The main component to achieve this goal was a full integration of all the subsystems, mechanical, electrical, and software.

The mechanical system of the robot did not change very much this sprint.


We did not successfully completed all of our goals for Sprint 3. We were inable to integrate the computer vision, the software in charge of tracking a person, with the assembled mechanical and electrical systems.

During this sprint we learned about all the components that we need to focus on for our final product. Our goals for the final product are as follows:

  • Integrate a path planning algorithm.
  • Tidy and organize the electrical system.
  • Redesign chassis to hold components and so there is generally less wobble.
  • Design robot to look more like a crab.
  • More traction for the legs- make rubber feet.
